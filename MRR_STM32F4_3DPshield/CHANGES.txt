v0.1

- Development of prototype.
- Based on STM32F407VET6 development board available from Aliexpress.
- Pinout based on current (2018-12-8) pins_STM32F4.h in Marlin firmware (bugsfix-2.0.x branch).
- 5 stepper drivers (X, Y, Z, E0, E1), two hot ends (E0, E1), two part cooling fans, single heat bed.
